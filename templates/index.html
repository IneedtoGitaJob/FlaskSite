Web Positivity

Takes in either a URL or a search prompt and displays either a color depending on how positive or negative the website is (Green is positive and red is negative) or a pie chart showing the positivity and negativity of a selection of News given a number of years in a range for a search prompt. This process can take up to ~20 seconds.
